COMMUNITY WORKS





🌍 Uniting for a Cleaner Coastline 🌊


On September 21, 2024, I joined an amazing group of passionate volunteers at Marina Beach, Chennai for The Planet’s Largest Coastal Cleanup 🧹. Alongside my team representing Rotaract Club of VIT (Chennai Campus), we joined forces with CommuniTree and Rotaract District 3234, all committed to preserving the beauty of our beaches and protecting marine life 🐢.


Even though we were drenched, our spirits stayed high! 🌦️ With trash bags in hand, we combed the beach, collecting waste that would otherwise endanger our oceans 🌊. Seeing everyone rally for this cause even in challenging weather was inspiring and reaffirmed the power of community 💪💚.


A huge shoutout to the organizers and every volunteer who showed up! 🌟 Every small step makes a difference, and I’m proud to have contributed to this impactful movement. Here’s to more hands joining together for a sustainable, cleaner future! 🌍🌱


🌸 One Walk, One Hope : Breast Cancer Awareness 🌸


On October 27, 2024, I had the honor of joining the "One Walk, One Hope" walkathon organized by the District Rotaract Council, Rotary International District 3234, and several Rotaract Clubs in Chennai. This event, held at Island Ground, Chennai, brought together people from all walks of life, united by one mission: to raise awareness for breast cancer.


Breast cancer awareness is more than just wearing pink or participating in an event—it's about creating lasting impact through education, early detection, and support for those affected. This walkathon was an inspiring reminder of how powerful community support can be in promoting these crucial messages and saving lives.


Receiving a certificate of participation was a proud moment, but more importantly, this experience allowed me to contribute to a cause that matters deeply to so many. I am grateful to the Rotary and Rotaract organizations for making events like these possible and for fostering a sense of responsibility and compassion in our communities.

Together, we can make a difference. 💪💖
